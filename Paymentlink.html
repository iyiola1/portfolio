BACKGROUND

CoralPay Payment Link is a no-code tool for creating payment links and invoices to collect payments easily.


Many small businesses and individuals struggle to collect payments online due to the complexity of setting up websites or integrating payment systems. To solve this, CoralPay created Payment Link—a no-code tool that lets users easily generate payment links and invoices without technical skills. It’s a simple, flexible, and secure way to collect payments digitally.

WHAT WE WANTED TO SOLVE

Building a Payment Link Platform


Small businesses, freelancers, and individuals often face barriers when trying to collect payments online. Setting up websites, handling integrations, or hiring developers can be expensive and time-consuming. Many existing solutions are too technical, limiting access for non-developers who need a simple way to request and receive payments.

Payment Collection Frustration for Businesses Businesses often struggle to collect payments online without building a website or dealing with complex integrations. Many lack the technical resources to set up traditional payment systems, leading to delays, missed opportunities, and inefficient processes.

WHAT WE DID

Understanding the needs of businesses


CoralPay Payment Link is a no-code, easy-to-use platform that empowers individuals and businesses to generate custom payment links and invoices—without the need for a website, coding skills, or complex technical setups. The solution is designed to streamline payment collection by offering flexibility through different link types, invoice generation, and a seamless user experience.

  • • Faster onboarding to help users start collecting payments quickly without technical barriers.
  • • Ensure better transparency in fees and rates to build trust and confidence.
  • • Deliver a trusted and scalable solution that supports growing businesses with reliable performance.
  • • Support multiple payment methodswith clearly labeled options for a seamless user experience.

Looking at the competition

To shape a seamless and competitive user experience for CoralPay Payment Link, I conducted a thorough analysis of similar solutions such as Paystack Payment Pages, Flutterwave Payment Links, and Monnify. This competitive research helped uncover gaps in customization, payment tracking, and ease of use. These insights validated our initial design direction and guided us in building a payment tool that meets real business needs—while simplifying the entire process.

DESIGN

The primary goal was to make the payment link creation process effortless, allowing merchants to generate, copy, and share links directly from the dashboard with ease. The layout was intentionally designed for speed and clarity enabling businesses to create payment links efficiently while displaying all essential details upfront.
The link details page provided users with a comprehensive view of the transaction, helping them make informed decisions and monitor activity. On the customer side, a clean and intuitive interface displayed item descriptions, pricing, and business branding—ensuring a smooth and trustworthy payment experience.

Key takeaways

Designing CoralPay Payment Link reinforced that building fintech solutions isn’t just about adding features—it’s about removing friction. Simplicity, transparency, and user trust guided every decision, from customizing payment links to designing a seamless invoicing experience. In the end, great UX wasn’t just a design goal it became a key enabler of business growth.
